- Narrative coherence

- trauma + morality  
- teleology + doctrine  
- tribal + aesthetic  
- purity + destiny

### **Stage 7 — Hyperfold Formation (Global Attractor)**  
- The hyperfold organizes other folds  
- It becomes the lens through which reality is interpreted  
- It becomes self‑sealing  
- It becomes identity‑relevant  
- It becomes emotionally protected

### **Stage 8 — Metastasis (Domain Expansion)**  
The hyperfold spreads into:

- politics  
- relationships  
- self‑concept  
- morality  
- future expectations  
- interpretation of others

### **Stage 9 — Stress & Contradiction (Anomaly Accumulation)**  
- Reality pushes back  
- Contradictions accumulate  
- Emotional cost rises  
- Cognitive dissonance increases  
- Social pressure shifts  

The hyperfold becomes brittle.

### **Stage 10 — Collapse or Inversion**  
Two outcomes:

**Collapse:**  
- The hyperfold loses coherence  
- The manifold reopens  
- Identity detaches  
- New trajectories become possible  

**Inversion:**  
- The hyperfold flips into its opposite  
- Same topology, reversed polarity  
- (e.g., zealot → anti‑zealot with identical rigidity)  

This is the “rebirth” or “implosion” stage.
